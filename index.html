DaScH-LAB

Data Sciences for Health @ TLS

Data Science for Health (DaScH) Laboratory is the Toscana Life Sciences research unit for the application of data sciences and new technologies at the service of scientific research. The DaScH-Lab can rely on the expertise of a diverse group of computational and machine-learning scientists working at the interface with experimental teams on a wide range of life science challenges.
